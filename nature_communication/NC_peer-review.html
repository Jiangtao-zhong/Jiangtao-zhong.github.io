NC_peer-review

我们花费大量的时间研究了《Nature Communications》的评审意见结构，并将其大致模型进行了抽象和总结。

在这里，我们将向您展示《Nature Communications》期刊上的开放同行评审意见的结构。

希望能够帮助您进一步了解《Nature Communications》的评审意见。

简介

进入同行评审阶段后，首先第 一轮会有2-4位评审进行评审，评审给出accept、minor、major 或 reject，其中 accept 即该 评审通过，reject 即该 评审拒绝，下一轮该评审都不再继续评议（一般首轮评审都会给出修改 意见，很少直接通过或拒绝）；而 minor 和 major 为小修和大修，作者根据评审意见进行修改后 ,将修改后 paper 及作者回复经 NC 编辑一并送由该评审进入下一轮评审。

经此多轮评审直至所有 评审给出通过或拒绝（评审轮次一般为 1-4 轮 ） 。

author与reviewer

Reviewer #1 :

This manuscript reported that high dose of GalNAc-siRNA induced toxicity through off-target effect of RNAi without effects of nucleic acids modifications. The results were proved by using a variety of new methodologies such as 5’phospholilation inhibition, REVERSIR, Seed swapping and summarized systematically. I believe that this study will give a significant impact on molecular design of siRNA based therapy for clinical application, which matches the quality of Nature Communications. However, following points should be carefully considered before this manuscript has been accepted for publication by Nature Communication.

                                                                                                                        response to Reviewer #1:
The authors appreciate the overall positive response of the reviewer and the valuable suggestions, which have been made. We have addressed the specific points raised, as follows:
Reviewer #1 :

1) Authors are advised to describe target genes for siRNA1 – 7.

                                                                                                                       response to Reviewer #1:
We added the target gene names and accession numbers for all siRNAs used in this manuscript to Supplementary Figure 1.
Reviewer #1 :

2) Authors are advised to describe capping structure of RISC loading block in the Figure.

                                                                                                                       response to Reviewer #1:
We added the structures of the residues used to block the 5’-end and prevent phosphorylation that were utilized in this study and updated Figure 1 accordingly.
Reviewer #1 :

3) Authors should apply statistical analysis and discus them in discussion.

                                                                                                                       response to Reviewer #1:
Differences between group means were evaluated for statistical significance using one-way ANOVA in GraphPad Prism 7 for all clinical pathology data. p-values are included in all Figures, Figure Legends, and described in the “Clinical pathology” section of Materials and Methods on page 7.
